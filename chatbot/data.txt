## **TECHNICAL ISSUES & TROUBLESHOOTING**

**Common Problems:**

1. **Can't download ebook? **
   - Check your internet connection
   - Try a different browser
   - Clear your browser cache
   - Contact support if problem persists

2. **App keeps crashing?**
   - Update the app to latest version
   - Restart your device
   - Check available storage space
   - Uninstall and reinstall if needed

3. **Ebook won't open on my e-reader?**
   - Ensure correct file format (EPUB for most readers)
   - Check your device compatibility
   - Try a different e-reader app
   - Contact support with device details

4. **Can't login to my account?**
   - Verify your internet connection
   - Check that Caps Lock is off
   - Use "Forgot Password" to reset
   - Clear cookies and try again

## **FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

**Q: Is BookStack available worldwide?**
A: We currently serve readers in 150+ countries.  Payment in your local currency available in most regions.

**Q: Can I share purchased ebooks? **
A:  Ebooks are licensed for personal use only.  Sharing is prohibited under our terms of service to protect authors' rights.

**Q: Do you offer physical books?**
A: No, BookStack specializes exclusively in digital ebooks. However, we have thousands of titles available. 

**Q: Can I return an ebook?**
A: Yes! You have 14 days to request a refund if you're unsatisfied (with conditions).

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A: Log into your account, go to "Payment Settings," and add or update your payment information.

**Q: Are ebooks DRM-protected?**
A: Most of our ebooks are DRM-free, allowing you to read on any device.  Some publisher titles may have DRM. 

**Q: Can I read offline?**
A: Yes! Download the EPUB or PDF version to read offline on your device.

**Q: How many devices can I use my account on?**
A: Your account can be accessed on unlimited devices simultaneously.
