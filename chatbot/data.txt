## **TECHNICAL ISSUES & TROUBLESHOOTING**

**Common Problems:**

1. **Can't download ebook?**
   - Ensure you're logged into your account
   - Check your internet connection is stable
   - Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
   - Clear your browser cache and cookies
   - Disable popup blockers temporarily
   - Check if file downloaded to your Downloads folder
   - Contact support via email if problem persists

2. **Can't login to my account?**
   - Verify your internet connection
   - Check that Caps Lock is OFF
   - Ensure you're using the correct email address
   - Use "Forgot Password" to reset your password
   - Clear browser cookies and try again
   - Try a different browser or device
   - Contact support if still unable to login

3. **Account not verified yet?**
   - Go to "Request Verification" page
   - Choose Email or SMS verification
   - Follow the verification instructions
   - Admin reviews within 24-48 hours
   - Contact support if verification is delayed

4. **Payment not going through?**
   - Ensure PayPal account has sufficient funds
   - Verify card details are correct in PayPal
   - Check for any PayPal restrictions or limits
   - Try a different payment method in PayPal
   - Contact PayPal support for payment gateway issues
   - Contact BookStack support with order details

5. **Voucher code not working?**
   - Check if voucher has expired
   - Verify minimum purchase amount is met
   - Ensure code is entered correctly (case-sensitive)
   - Check if voucher has usage limits
   - View "My Vouchers" for available codes
   - Contact support with voucher code details

6. **Can't find purchased ebook?**
   - Go to "My E-Books" page
   - Ensure you're logged into correct account
   - Check "Orders" page for purchase confirmation
   - Verify payment was completed successfully
   - Wait a few minutes and refresh page
   - Contact support with order number

## **FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS**

**Q: Is BookStack available worldwide?**
A: Yes! BookStack is accessible globally. Our website serves readers worldwide with secure PayPal payment processing available in most countries.

**Q: Can I share purchased ebooks with friends?**
A: No. Ebooks are licensed for personal use only. Sharing or distributing purchased content violates our terms of service and infringes on author copyrights. Each user must purchase their own copy.

**Q: Do you offer physical books?**
A: No, BookStack specializes exclusively in digital ebooks in PDF format. This allows instant delivery, lifetime access, and compatibility with all devices.

**Q: Can I return or refund an ebook?**
A: Due to the instant digital nature of our products, all sales are final once the ebook is downloaded. Please review book descriptions carefully before purchasing. Contact support for exceptional circumstances.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A: Payment is processed through PayPal. You can manage your payment methods directly in your PayPal account. BookStack does not store payment information.

**Q: Are ebooks DRM-protected?**
A: Our ebooks are in standard PDF format without DRM protection. This allows you to read on any device. However, redistribution is prohibited by our terms of service.

**Q: Can I read ebooks offline?**
A: Yes! Download the PDF files to your device and read them offline anytime using any PDF reader application. No internet connection required after download.

**Q: How many devices can I use?**
A: You can download and read your purchased ebooks on unlimited devices. Simply log into your account from any device and download your books.

**Q: Do I need a special app to read the ebooks?**
A: No special app needed! Our ebooks are in PDF format which can be opened with any PDF reader like Adobe Acrobat Reader, browser PDF viewers, or mobile PDF apps.

**Q: Will I lose access to my ebooks?**
A: No! Once purchased, you have lifetime access. You can download your ebooks anytime from your account, even years after purchase.

**Q: Can I re-download purchased ebooks?**
A: Absolutely! Go to "My E-Books" page and download your books as many times as you need. There are no download limits.

**Q: What if I forgot my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the password reset instructions sent to your email.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Go to "Request Verification" page, choose Email or SMS verification method, and follow the instructions. Admin will review and approve your verification request.

**Q: When will my account be verified?**
A: Email verifications are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours. SMS verifications with correct codes are approved automatically or within a few hours.

**Q: How do I get discount vouchers?**
A: Vouchers are provided to registered and verified users. Check "My Vouchers" page regularly for available discount codes. Special vouchers may be sent via email or SMS.

**Q: Can I use multiple vouchers on one order?**
A: Currently, only one voucher code can be applied per order. Choose the voucher that gives you the best discount.

**Q: Why can't I purchase ebooks?**
A: Your account must be verified before making purchases. Complete the account verification process first by going to "Request Verification" page.

**Q: How do I contact customer support?**
A: Use the contact form on our website. Fill in your details, describe your issue, and choose Email or Phone as your preferred contact method. Admin will respond within 24-48 hours.

**Q: What information should I include when contacting support?**
A: Include your registered email, order number (if applicable), detailed description of the issue, and any error messages or screenshots. This helps us assist you faster.

**Q: Can I change my registered email or phone number?**
A: Yes! Go to your "Profile" page and update your contact information. Make sure to use a valid email and phone number for support communications.
